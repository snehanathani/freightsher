About Us

Established in 1994, S.S. Shipping Agencies started operations at Kandla and Mundra Ports. With an expanding footprint, we're now operational in Ahmedabad. Over the years, we’ve earned a reputation for integrity and efficiency with port and customs authorities, offering expertise in handling a wide variety of cargo across India and internationally.

Our Services

Customs Clearance

Efficient clearance services at major Gujarat ports like Kandla, Mundra, Pipavav, and Ahmedabad ICD.

Documentation

Comprehensive support including duty drawbacks, IGST refunds, and DGFT licensing.

Freight Forwarding

Global freight forwarding via top shipping liners with competitive rates and wide coverage.

Transportation

Fleet of trucks and reefers for efficient inland cargo movement and just-in-time delivery.

Customer Support

Daily status reports, customized logistics support, and DDU/DDP capabilities for global shipments.
